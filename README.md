# 🎓 University of Prince Mugrin – Career Analyst Dashboard Portfolio

Welcome to my **Career Analyst Dashboard Portfolio**!
I’m currently working as a **Career Analyst** at the **University of Prince Mugrin**, where I develop interactive dashboards to support data-driven decision-making across the university.

This repository highlights my work in analyzing and visualizing key university data to:

* Optimize institutional resources
* Support strategic planning
* Align academic programs with labor market demands

---

## 📊 What’s Inside

This portfolio features a series of dashboards covering a wide range of topics, including:

* 🔍 **Labor Market Insights** – Regional demand trends and in-demand skills
* 💰 **Budget & Resource Allocation** – Tracking spending and planning needs
* 🖥️ **Lab & Facility Utilization** – Monitoring on-campus resource use
* 🎓 **Student Demographics & Performance** – Analyzing student profiles and success metrics
* 🎯 **Alumni Outcomes** – Employment trends and post-graduation trajectories
* 📈 **Strategic Growth Opportunities** – Identifying challenges and areas for development

Each dashboard is designed to present clear, actionable insights through intuitive and interactive visualizations.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

I leverage a range of modern tools to build, automate, and maintain the dashboards:

* **Power BI** – For data modeling, visualization, and dashboard design
* **Microsoft Power Platform**:

  * **Power Apps** – Custom applications for interacting with data
  * **Power Automate** – Workflow automation
  * **Dataverse** – Unified data storage
* **Microsoft Fabric** – Data integration and analytics platform
* **Web Scraping Tools** – For collecting real-time external data
